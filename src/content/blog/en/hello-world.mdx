# Hello, world

After years of shipping Chrome extensions and scattering notes across half a dozen places, I finally took a weekend to build a proper home on the web: **hi.kyriewen.cn**.

## What lives here

- **Products** — Every Chrome extension and web app I keep iterating on.
- **Writing** — Technical notes, indie-hacking reflections, both in English and 中文.
- **Now** — What I am focused on _right now_, not what I did last year.
- **Stats** — Build-in-public numbers: GitHub stars, Chrome users, subscribers.
- **Guestbook** — Leave a note, say hi.

## Why bother?

> _Your website is the one piece of real estate you fully own on the internet._

Social networks come and go. Twitter rebrands, Medium paywalls, RSS dies and comes back. But a simple Next.js site on a domain I pay $10/year for? That is mine.

## What is next

Writing more. Shipping more. Making the stats dashboard embarrassingly honest.
